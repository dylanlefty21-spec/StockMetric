The three scores

One number that tells the whole story

Every score is 0–100. Every score has a color. Every color has a meaning.

📈
Trend Score
Is this stock going up or down? We compare the price against its 20, 50, and 200-day averages and check if buyers are stronger than sellers.
🛒
Buy Timing Score
Is right now a good moment to buy? Even great stocks can be temporarily overbought. This score finds the best entry windows using RSI, MACD, and volume.
🛡️
Risk Score
How volatile and stable is this stock? A high score means calm and predictable. A low score means it swings wildly — higher potential gains and losses.
Green (60–100) — Strong signal
Yellow (40–59) — Mixed signals
Red (0–39) — Weak signal
